How it works

Your squad is three swipes away

🎮

Build your profile

Add your games, verify your rank via official APIs, and set your playstyle. No fake stats — everything is verified.

⚡

Swipe to match

We show you players who share your games and region. Swipe right to squad up, left to pass. Simple as that.

💬

Features

Built for real players

Verified

Real ranks, real stats

We verify hours and rank directly from Steam, Riot, Bungie, and Tracker Network. Every stat you see is pulled from official sources.

Sessions

Host or join sessions

Post what you're playing and when. Players RSVP straight from your profile card. No Discord ping needed.

Reputation

Rate your squadmates

After every session, rate your teammates. Good players rise. Toxic players get filtered out naturally.

Privacy

Your region, not your location

We only show your general region to other players. Your exact location is never shared or stored.
