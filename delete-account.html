Delete Your SquadUp Account

You can delete your SquadUp account at any time. There are two ways to do this:

Option 1: Delete from within the app

  1. Open the SquadUp app
  2. Tap the Settings icon in the top right corner
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete account
  4. Confirm deletion when prompted

Your account, profile, matches, and messages will be permanently deleted immediately.

Option 2: Request deletion by email

If you no longer have access to the app, email us at:

squadupggworks@gmail.com

Include the email address associated with your account and the subject line "Account Deletion Request". We will process your request within 7 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Your profile information including name, photo, bio, and game list
  • All matches and squad connections
  • All messages and chat history
  • Your swipe history and preferences

What may be retained

  • Anonymous, aggregated usage data that cannot identify you
  • Records required for legal compliance, fraud prevention, or safety purposes (retained for up to 90 days)
